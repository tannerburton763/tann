About the Course

This is real estate investing for beginners, a step-by-step system to determine if you have a viable deal.

As a real estate investor you are constantly shown deals from brokers. But, how do you know if you have a good deal worth pursuing or just another bad deal that you should quickly pass on?

In this course, Ben Leybovich will teach you the essential financial principles of real estate investing, which will serve as a foundation for any future deal you review.
From there, Ben will take you through a step-by-step system that you can apply to any multi-family deal to determine if you have a viable deal or not.

What You Will Learn

  • How to view real estate investing from a financial point of view
  • What real estate metrics you should pay attention to
  • Understand what is desirable from a tenant’s point of view
  • 13 steps you can follow that will tell you if you have a good deal
  • Market analysis and Property analysis
  • A real life case study that walks you through the important aspects of a deal

Learn How to Analyze a Deal

What you will be able to do by the end of the course

You will have an understanding about real estate and finance that you can apply to any deal that comes your way in the future. You will be able to quickly analyze a deal and understand if the deal is worth pursuing or not.

Who should take this course?

Any investor who is uncertain about how to analyze a deal properly. Also, this course is perfect for anyone who wishes to learn more about real estate finance.
